Hey! I'm Vincent Dell'Arciprete, and I've been pursuing the practice of various kinds of digital and drawn art for the last 8 years of my life. I have a great appreciation for science fiction and fantasy-based works of all sorts, and aside from art, I have hobby level interests in games, astronomy, music, and history.

My work that I've chosen to put on display is a collection of my various works and projects I've created throughout my time in high school and college, and in my free time.

I hope you enjoy what you'll be seeing!
